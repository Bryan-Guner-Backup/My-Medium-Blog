How to make your own copy

  1. Once inside the template, look to the right of the board and find the menu. Click on More.

2. Click on Copy Board. This will make a copy to your own personal Trello account, so as to not overwrite the master template. Title your copy with the name of your team and set the board to Public. This is to ensure that SL's and staff can review your progress and also saves you the trouble of having to invite all of your students directly.

Ensure that Keep Cards and Keep Template Cards are both checked and click on Create.
