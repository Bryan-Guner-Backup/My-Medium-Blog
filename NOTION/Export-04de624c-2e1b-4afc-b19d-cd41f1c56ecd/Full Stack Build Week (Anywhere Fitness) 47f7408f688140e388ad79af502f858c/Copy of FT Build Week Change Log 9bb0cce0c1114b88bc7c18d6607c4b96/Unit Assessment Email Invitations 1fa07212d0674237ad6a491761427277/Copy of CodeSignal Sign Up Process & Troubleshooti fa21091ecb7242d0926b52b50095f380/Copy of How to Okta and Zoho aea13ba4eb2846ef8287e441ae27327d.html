Copy of How to Okta and Zoho

First time login

Step 1 - Getting an email from Okta:

You’ll receive an email, in your personal email account, from Okta inviting you to activate your account. This email will include a link, that is good for 7 days, to activate your Okta account.

Step 2 - Activating your Okta account:

  1. Choose a password. Please pay attention to the password complexity requirements.
  1. Choose a password reset question and answer.
  1. Add a phone number for password reset via SMS (optional but recommended)
  1. Choose a picture to see when you log in to Okta.
  1. Click “Create My Account”.

Step 3 - Arrive at your Okta dashboard:

After activating your account you’ll arrive at your Okta dashboard. You’ll see one application there, Zoho Mail, as below.

Step 4 - Login to Zoho via Okta:

Clicking the link for the Zoho Mail application will take you to the main Zoho mail page. Click the “LOGIN” link in the upper right corner.

Step 5 - Type your lambdastudents.com email address:

Enter your @lambdastudents.com email address and click “Next”.

Step 6 - Arrive at your Zoho mailbox:

The first time your open your mailbox you’ll see a slideshow with info on how to use it. You can view or skip this.

After viewing or skipping the slideshow you’ll see your mailbox.

Subsequent logins

After first-time login and account set up there are 2 ways you can access your Zoho mailbox.

Option 1 - Login to Okta:

You can login to Okta at https://lambdaschoolsso.okta.com.

After logging in you’ll see your Okta dashboard and can click Zoho from there.

Option 2 - Login to Zoho directly:

You can login to your Zoho mail directly at https://mail.zoho.com. Click the login link in the upper right corner of Zoho mail webpage.

On this page you’ll enter your lambdastudents.com email address.

If you are not logged in to Okta already, you’ll be redirected to the Okta login page. Login here with your lambdastudents.com email and Okta password.

After logging in you’ll be at your Zoho mailbox.
